Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 2024

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the "Imposter" mobile application ("the App").

1. Information We Collect

We believe in data minimization. We only collect the information necessary to provide and improve the App's core functionalities.

  • Usage Data and Analytics: We collect aggregated, anonymous data regarding app performance, crash reports, and basic usage statistics to improve game stability.
  • No PII Collection: We do NOT require, collect, or store Personally Identifiable Information (PII) such as your name, address, or phone number to play the core game.

2. Third-Party Services and AI Generation

To provide dynamic word and question generation, the App integrates with third-party Artificial Intelligence APIs (including Google Gemini).

  • Anonymized Prompts: When generating custom game packs, non-personal parameters (like selected category or difficulty) are sent to our AI providers. No personal identifying data is ever transmitted in these requests.
  • The content generated is used transiently for gameplay and is not stored permanently on external servers linked to your identity.

3. In-App Purchases and Subscriptions

The App offers Premium AI access via auto-renewing subscriptions processed securely through Apple App Store, Google Play Store, and managed via RevenueCat.

  • We do not collect or have access to your raw payment details (such as credit card numbers).
  • Purchase history is tied to an anonymous App User ID to unlock premium features across your devices using the same platform account.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect any anonymous data collected against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

support@impostorapp.com
