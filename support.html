Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed directly by your device's app store. Go to your Apple App Store or Google Play Store account settings, navigate to "Subscriptions", and you can cancel auto-renewal from there.

How does Local Multiplayer (P2P) work?

Make sure all devices are connected to the exact same Wi-Fi network. One player taps "Host Game", and the others can join by entering the short room code displayed on the host's screen.

Will the AI generate inappropriate words?

We use strict safety filters and context prompts to ensure AI generation is safe and fun. If you enable the specific "Family-Friendly Mode," the filters are increased significantly to only allow completely safe packs.
